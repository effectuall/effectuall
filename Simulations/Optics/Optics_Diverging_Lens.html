Image Formation with Converging Lens

Description

For simulation of image formation in concave lenses. Use the control menu, to change the lens focus, to move the object and adjust the object height.

Observe that the focal length is negative here.

Lens Formula

$$\frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v}-\frac{1}{u} $$ \[\text{or}\] $$\frac{1}{v} = \frac{1}{f}+\frac{1}{u} $$

    ➤ Focal length of concave lens (diverging lens) are negative.
    ➤ Image through the concave lens is always virtual and erect.

Magnification

$$m=\frac{v}{u} $$
    ➤ Magnification is less than is 1.
